What is the purpose of the website

This website is the stage that showcases the brand I am creating. It is the place where the audience can find the most about the brand and collections being made. At certain stage in the future, the website will build the eshop as well, selling the garment pieces according to the season.

Who is the intended audience?

The audience will mostly be my customers, buyers and other people who relate to fashion, either in terms of their jobs and interests.

What tone do you want for the siteâ€™s written content?

The site won't have too much of the written content, since it is the display of my collection. The tone of the visual is set to better deliver the mood of my brand.

What will be the primary content of your site?

The parimary content will include the landing page, which are pictures dispalys the mood of the collection from that certain season. It directs the audience directly to the new season. Besides, there are also past seasons that can be found under the menu. Then, it is the shop.

What will be the secondary content?

The secondary content will be the extra information that audience want to know, such as "About the Designer", "Contact Us" and the brand on social media. For the online shop, we will have the customer service and related policies on "shipping and returns".

How will users navigate through your site?

The navigation is straightforward. They can just follow the menu, then they will find the content easily. They can always go back or jump to the next content by clicking the menu. I will keep the menu's options simple, so they understand it at the second when they see it.
